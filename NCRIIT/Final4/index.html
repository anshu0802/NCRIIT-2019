What is NCRIIT?

01 Platform

NCRIIT

The National Conference on Recent Trends and Innovation in Information Technology (NCRIIT), conducted by SRM Institute of Science and Technology is scheduled to be held on 3rd April 2019. The focus of the conference will be on exploring diverse ideas with due appreciation of recent developments that will have far-reaching consequences. Our esteemed panel of judges are from reputed institutions with very high level of expertise. More than a 100 scholars and students will be in attendance to showcase their contributions to the exciting times that lie ahead. Considering how disruptive IT has been in the last decade and given ample proof how much more disruptive it is going to be, National Conferences like these are aimed at nurturing a competitive temperament in students.

ABOUT SRM

‘ Education is all about creating an environment of academic freedom- where bright minds meet, discover and learn to lead.’ SRM Institute of Science and Technology works towards complete justice to the statement which has led to the institute being at the top of university rankings with over 38,000 students and over 2600 faculty members employed across all the campuses, offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes in Engineering, Management, Medicine and Science & Humanities. Reputed members of the academicia from International universities, flexible and dynamic curriculum which aids the students in keeping pace with the latest cutting edge technologies, coupled with global opportunities and state-of-the-art research facilities are what sets our institute apart.
